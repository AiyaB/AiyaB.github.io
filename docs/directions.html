  • To The Nearest Groceries

    Closest Shop: Market Cepani

    Go left when leaving the hostel. Continue on up the steep hill, turning left once at the top. Just a short ways on the left is the cornerstore. It's great for any basic items like milk and fruit.

    Closest Supermarket: Planet

    Go left when leaving the hostel. Continue on up the steep hill, turning right at the top. Follow the road (Rruga Gj Rgj Raniti) until you see the red supermarket sign on the left side. Planet is perfect when you want a wider range of food, or want a particular drink.
